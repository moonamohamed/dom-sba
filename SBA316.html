Let’s Share the Weight In silence, we carry a weight unknown,
A quiet burden, a seed that’s grown,
A storm inside that no one sees,
Yet it winds through the heart like a restless breeze.
But if we spoke, would the burden break?
Could we find the courage to unmake
The walls we build in quiet fear,
Hoping no one will draw too near?
What if we shared? Just one small word,
A whisper in the wind, quietly heard.
Not for pity, not for shame,
But to know we’re not alone in this pain.
So here’s my hand, reaching out to you,
To share the weight and help it through.
There’s strength in the stories that we hide,
In the broken parts we try to deny.
Together, let’s shatter the walls we’ve built,
And find the healing that comes with the tilt Of an open heart, no more alone,
Let’s share the weight, and make it our own.
